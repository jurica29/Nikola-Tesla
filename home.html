1887

Tesla Re-imagines The Electric Motor

In 1887, Tesla came up with an induction motor that was operated by alternating current, a form of power that was becoming common in Europe and the USA. This type of power provided various benefits, especially in long-distance power transmission.

1881

Tesla Goes To Work For Edison

In 1882, he found another job in Paris with a company referred to as the Continental Edison Company. The management team noticed his abilities and soon introduced him as part of the team that would produce dynamos and motors.

1943

Nikola Tesla is born

Nikola Tesla was born in Smiljan, Croatia. He is believed to have been born at midnight. During his birth, lightning struck during a summer storm.
